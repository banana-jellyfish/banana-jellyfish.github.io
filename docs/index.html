Facts about Manta Rays!!!

Here are some facts about manta rays:

  • Manta rays are the largest species of rays in the world.
  • They can grow up to 29 feet (8.8 meters) in width.
  • Manta rays are filter feeders, primarily eating plankton.

Here are some more interesting facts:

  1. They can fly out of the water
  2. They are on average bigger than a car
  3. They have the largest brain relative to body size of any fish
